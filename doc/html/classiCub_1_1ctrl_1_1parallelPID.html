Detailed Description

General structure of parallel (non-interactive) PID.

u = sat(P + I + D)

Components expressed in Laplace transform:

  • \( e_p=W_p*ref-fb \)
  • \( e_i=W_i*ref-fb \)
  • \( e_d=W_d*ref-fb \)
  • \( P=K_p*e_p \)
  • \( I=(K_i*e_i+(sat(u)-u)/T_t)/s \)
  • \( D=K_d*e_d \cdot s/(1+s \cdot T_d/N); \quad [T_d=K_d/K_p] \)
