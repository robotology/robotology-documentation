\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
General structure of parallel (non-\/interactive) PID. 

u = sat(P + I + D)

Components expressed in Laplace transform\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $ e_p=W_p*ref-fb $
\item $ e_i=W_i*ref-fb $
\item $ e_d=W_d*ref-fb $
\item $ P=K_p*e_p $
\item $ I=(K_i*e_i+(sat(u)-u)/T_t)/s $
\item $ D=K_d*e_d \cdot s/(1+s \cdot T_d/N); \quad [T_d=K_d/K_p] $ 
\end{DoxyItemize}
