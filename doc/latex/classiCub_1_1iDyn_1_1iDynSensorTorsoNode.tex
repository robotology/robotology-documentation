\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class for connecting a central-\/up limb, a left and right limb of the \mbox{\hyperlink{namespaceiCub}{i\+Cub}}, and exchanging kinematic and wrench information between limbs, when both left/right limb have FT sensors and the central-\/up one use the kinematic and wrench information coming from a inertial measurements or another \mbox{\hyperlink{classiCub_1_1iDyn_1_1iDynSensorNode}{i\+Dyn\+Sensor\+Node}}. 

This is the base class of Upper\+Torso and Lower\+Torso. The connection between Upper\+Torso and Lower\+Torso is not handled here\+: it is simply supposed that a Torso Node receives correct kinematic and wrench input from outside.
