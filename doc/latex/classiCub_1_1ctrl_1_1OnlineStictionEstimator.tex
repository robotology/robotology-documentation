\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Online Stiction Estimator. 

Estimate the up and down stiction values. ~\newline
 During the experiment, the joint is controlled by a high-\/level pid controller that commands directly the voltage in order to track a time varying reference position. The stiction values are estimated during the rising and falling edge transitions.

Available options are\+:

{\bfseries{joint}} $<$int$>$\+: specify the joint to be controlled.

{\bfseries{Ts}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the estimator sample time given in seconds.

{\bfseries{T}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the period in seconds of the reference waveform used for tracking.

{\bfseries{Kp}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the proportional term of the high-\/level controller for tracking purpose.

{\bfseries{Ki}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the integral term of the high-\/level controller for tracking purpose.

{\bfseries{Kd}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the derivative term of the high-\/level controller for tracking purpose.

{\bfseries{vel\+\_\+thres}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the velocity threshold (in degrees) used to identify the estimation time windows. The estimation is carried out whenever $\vert$vel$\vert$$<$vel\+\_\+thres.

{\bfseries{e\+\_\+thres}} $<$double$>$\+: specify the error threshold (in degrees) above which keep updating the stiction values. The estimation is carried out until $\vert$e\+\_\+mean$\vert$$>$e\+\_\+thres, where e\+\_\+mean accounts for the integral average of the error computed within the estimation time window. Value given in degrees.

{\bfseries{gamma}} ($<$double$>$ $<$double$>$)\+: specify the gains used for updating the stiction up and down values, respectively.

{\bfseries{stiction}} ($<$double$>$ $<$double$>$)\+: specify the initial stiction up and down values, respectively.
