\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class for connecting torso, left and right leg of the \mbox{\hyperlink{namespaceiCub}{i\+Cub}}, and exchanging kinematic and wrench information between limbs, when both legs have FT sensors and the torso use the kinematic and wrench information coming from Upper\+Torso. 

The correct connection bewteen Upper\+Torso and Lower\+Torso is not handled here; it is supposed that Lower\+Torso receives correct kinematic and wrench variables for the initialization of the kinematic and wrench phases.
