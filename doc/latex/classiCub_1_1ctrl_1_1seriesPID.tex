\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
General structure of series (interactive) PID. 

u = sat((P + I) $\ast$ (1 + D) $\ast$ (ref-\/fb))

Components expressed in Laplace transform\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $ P=K_p $
\item $ I=K_p/(T_i*s) $
\item $ D=K_d \cdot s/(1+s \cdot T_d/N); \quad [T_d=K_d/K_p] $ 
\end{DoxyItemize}
