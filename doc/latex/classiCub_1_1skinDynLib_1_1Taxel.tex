\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class that encloses everything relate to a \mbox{\hyperlink{classiCub_1_1skinDynLib_1_1Taxel}{Taxel}}, i.\+e. 

the atomic element the \mbox{\hyperlink{namespaceiCub}{i\+Cub}} skin is composed of. It is empowered by a Position and a Normal (both relative to the body part it belong to), and some more useful members (such as its 2D coordinates into the image frame of one of the eyes, its Frame of Reference base on the Position and Normal members, its position into the World Reference Frame)
