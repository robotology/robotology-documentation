\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class for computing force/moment of the FT sensor placed in the middle of the \mbox{\hyperlink{namespaceiCub}{i\+Cub}}\textquotesingle{}s left or right arm. 

The sensor parameters are automatically set by chosing left or right during initialization of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classiCub_1_1iDyn_1_1iCubArmDyn}{i\+Cub\+Arm\+Dyn}}.
