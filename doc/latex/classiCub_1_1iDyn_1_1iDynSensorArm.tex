\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class for computing joint force/moment/torque of an \mbox{\hyperlink{namespaceiCub}{i\+Cub}} arm (left/right) given the FT measurements of the sensor placed in the middle of the arm. 

The sensor parameters are automatically set by chosing left or right during initialization of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classiCub_1_1iDyn_1_1iCubArmDyn}{i\+Cub\+Arm\+Dyn}}.
