\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Online Compensator Design. 

Tune in an online fashion a controller for a DC motor plant identified by means of \mbox{\hyperlink{classiCub_1_1ctrl_1_1OnlineDCMotorEstimator}{Online\+DCMotor\+Estimator}} .

The design of the controller is such that the properties of the compensated closed-\/loop system comply with specifications given in terms of bandwidth and disturbance rejection.

This class has four operative modes\+: one for the plant estimation, one for the plant validation, one for the stiction estimation and one for validating the controller\textquotesingle{}s design.
